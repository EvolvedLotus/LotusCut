# LotusCut V1

![LotusCut Logo](./assets/icon.png)

### Video editing as easy as editing a document.

LotusCut V1 is a professional-grade desktop application that transforms your videos into editable text. By syncing transcripts to the video timeline, it allows you to edit footage by simply deleting or moving text.

---

## 🌟 What is LotusCut?

LotusCut is a powerful Non-Linear Editor (NLE) built for the AI era. Instead of scrubbing through hours of footage manually, LotusCut uses **OpenAI’s Whisper** to transcribe your media entirely offline. Once transcribed, you can cut your video exactly like you edit a word document—cut a sentence from the transcript, and the corresponding video segment is instantly removed.

### Core Workflows:
*   **Text-Based Editing:** Import local files (MP4, MKV, MOV) and edit via the transcript.
*   **YouTube Integration:** Paste a link to transcribing and edit YouTube videos directly within the app.
*   **Auto-Editor:** Automatically strips long pauses and silence from your recordings using intelligent dead-air detection.

## 🎯 Who is this for?

LotusCut was designed specifically for creators who need to move fast:

*   **YouTubers & Content Creators:** Quickly trim "talking head" videos or podcast clips.
*   **Podcasters:** Find specific quotes or segments in long-form recordings instantly.
*   **Editors:** Draft rough cuts in minutes rather than hours before moving to high-level grading.
*   **Social Media Managers:** Extract highlights and clips from long streams or meetings.

## 📥 Getting Started

LotusCut runs as a standalone installer for Windows. 

1.  **Download:** Head to the [Official Releases](https://github.com/EvolvedLotus/LotusCut/releases) page and download `LotusCut-Setup-1.0.0.exe`.
2.  **Install:** Run the setup wizard on your Windows machine.
3.  **Setup AI Engine:** On the first launch, the app will automatically configure the local AI engine. You’ll see a progress bar for "Setting up AI Engine"—this ensures the models run optimally on your specific CPU/GPU.

## 🛠️ Security & Privacy

Privacy is not an afterthought; it’s the default.
*   **100% Offline AI:** All transcription happens on your hardware. We never upload your audio, video, or data to any cloud.
*   **Sandboxed Environment:** Built on Electron with native security isolation, ensuring the app remains secure and isolated from your system files.
